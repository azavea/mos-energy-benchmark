About this tool

This visualization tool provides building-level energy performance data for the {{ ::yearsString }} calendar years for Philadelphia’s largest commercial and multifamily buildings, including {{ ::info.reporting_properties }} buildings required to report annual energy and water usage as part of Philadelphia’s energy benchmarking law. You can learn more about energy benchmarking at {{ ::getPrettyUrl(info.benchmarking_url) }}.


Building Energy Performance

Building energy performance is a critical metric for cities dedicated to reducing greenhouse gas emissions. In Philadelphia, {{ ::info.greenhouse_gas_emissions }} of citywide greenhouse gas emissions stem from building energy use. As part of Greenworks: A Vision for a Sustainable Philadelphia, Philadelphia's sustainability framework, Mayor {{ ::info.mayor_name }} set a goal of {{ ::info.sustainability_plan_goal }}. Improving building performance will help Philadelphia reach that goal, and benchmarking provides building owners with the information they need to begin making informed decisions about upgrading and investing in energy efficiency (see this page for existing resources to invest in your facility).
